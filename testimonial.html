    Deep Money

    Deep Money is a Punjabi singer from India. He was born on 24th November 1986 in Delhi, India. Deep Money's real name is Amandeep Singh.

    Milind Gaba

    Millind Gaba, popularly known by his stage name Music MG, is an Indian musician, rapper, singer-songwriter, composer and actor. Well known for his work in Punjabi cinema and Bollywood, he has served as a music director in movies like Dilliwali Zaalim Girlfriend (2015), Welcome Back (2015) and Housefull3.

    Dilbagh Singh is a Punjabi language singer-songwriter and film actor. Singh was born in Delhi, but is currently based in Mumbai, India.
